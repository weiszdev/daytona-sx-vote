Roczen Is On Fire — Three Straight Wins and Climbing

Ken Roczen dominated St. Louis wire-to-wire, winning by 13.2 seconds over Justin Cooper. That's three consecutive victories and his third win of 2026. Nobody in the field has looked this fast for this long this season.

He entered the year as a question mark after switching to Suzuki Factory. Now he's the hottest rider on the planet, 5 points off the lead with 5 rounds remaining. If he wins Nashville, he takes the red plate.

Shimoda Out for the Season — 250 East Is Now a Two-Horse Race

Jo Shimoda is done for the rest of Supercross with a fibula fracture. That reshuffles the entire 250 East title fight. Cole Davies now leads Seth Hammaker by 11 points with no third rider within striking distance.

Nashville is 250 East territory. Davies vs. Hammaker — head to head, no distractions, no safety net. Every point matters. Hammaker needs to close the gap tonight or risk watching Davies ride away with it.
